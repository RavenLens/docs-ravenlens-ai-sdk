# Tutorial Intro

Let's discover **RavenLens API in less than 5 minutes**.

### What is RavenLens?
RavenLens is a SSOT seamless platform for __tacking and improving your AI Systems__ can be either _LLM chats or AI Agents_

### Why should i use RavenLens?
If your client satisfaction and continues project imporvement is essential point RavenLens platform and RavenLens API are tailored for you

### What will be benefits of integrating my App with RavenLens?

Integrating your application with RavenLens provides several key advantages:

- **Single Source of Truth (SSOT)**: Centralize all your AI interactions and prompt versions in one place.
- **Enhanced Observability**: Track LLM performance, latency, and costs across different models and agents.
- **Continuous Improvement**: Use data-driven insights to refine your prompts and improve client satisfaction.
- **Seamless Integration**: Quick setup with our specialized SDKs for JavaScript, TypeScript, and more.
- **Collaboration**: Enable your team to manage, test, and deploy prompts without touching the codebase.
- **Benchmarking**: Choose the best prompt and model to given task regard: performance, perplexity, cost-to-output-quality
